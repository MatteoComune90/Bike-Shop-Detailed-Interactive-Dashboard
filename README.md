# Bike-Shop-Detailed-Interactive-Dashboard

**Overview**

This Power BI project analyzes the sales and operational performance of AdventureWorks, a multinational company that sells bicycles, accessories, and clothing. The report combines financial, product, and customer data to deliver a full picture of business performance.
It was built using data modeling, DAX measures, Power Query transformations, and interactive navigation to create a modern, insight-driven dashboard.
The report is divided into four main pages and one tooltip page, each designed for a specific analytical purpose.

**Executive Dashboard**

This page serves as the main performance overview for management and stakeholders. It focuses on company-wide KPIs and trends to track financial health.

**Key focus areas:**
- High-level metrics: €24.9M revenue, €10.5M profit, 25.2K orders, 2.2% return rate.
- Weekly and monthly trends showing revenue and order growth over time.
- Category-level performance across Bikes, Accessories, and Clothing.
- Top 10 products ranked by total revenue and return rate.
- Month-over-month comparisons for quick performance evaluation.

**Purpose:**
To provide executives with a clear, data-backed summary of company performance, identify revenue trends, and monitor product returns or category shifts.

**Map Page**
This page offers a global view of AdventureWorks’ sales performance and supports regional business analysis.

**Key focus areas:**
- Interactive map with revenue distribution across major markets.
- Regional tabs for Europe, North America, and Pacific.
- Country-level comparisons highlighting key markets such as the United States, Canada, Germany, France, and Australia.
- Easy identification of regional sales leaders and growth opportunities.

**Purpose:**
To evaluate market reach, identify high-performing regions, and support decisions about international strategy and marketing investment.

**Product Details**
This page explores product-level sales performance and tracks results against monthly business targets.

**Key focus areas:**
- KPIs for orders, revenue, and profit vs. target.
- Profit and return trend lines to monitor performance consistency.
- Filters for individual products to identify bestsellers or underperformers.
- Analysis of return rates to detect potential product quality or logistics issues.

**Purpose:**
To help product and finance teams understand profitability, sales efficiency, and product lifecycle performance, supporting inventory and pricing decisions.

**Customer Details**
This page provides an in-depth look at customer demographics and purchasing patterns.

**Key focus areas:**
- 17.4K total customers and an average revenue of €1,431 per customer.
- Trends in customer growth and average spend over time.
- Segmentation by income level and occupation group.
- Top 100 customers ranked by total orders and revenue.
- Individual customer highlight section showing total revenue and orders.

**Purpose:**
To give insight into who the customers are, how they spend, and which segments drive the highest revenue. This helps shape marketing and retention strategies.

**Category Tooltip**
An interactive tooltip designed to give on-hover insights across the report.

**Includes:**
- Key KPIs: revenue, profit, orders, returns, and return rate.
- A small weekly trend chart for context.

**Purpose:**
To enhance user experience by offering instant summary data without navigating away from the main view.


**Tools & Techniques**
- Power BI Desktop – dashboard creation and visualization
- Power Query – data cleaning and transformation
- DAX Measures – dynamic KPIs and business calculations
- Drill-through and Tooltips – enhanced interactivity
- Bookmarks & Page Navigation – multi-page dashboard experience
